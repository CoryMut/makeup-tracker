About


Have a rather large, disorganized collection of makeup?
Have you ever wanted a way to conveniently organize and keep track of that ever-growing collection?
Well now you can!

Currently, there is support for nearly 17,000 products when accounting for the different color options. Once logged in, adding a product to your collection is as easy as searching by name, brand, type of product (such as 'blush' or 'foundation') and clicking the "Add to Collection" button. That's it!

Once a product has been added, you will see lots of information about the product, including a link to purchase the product, the brand, type of product, last known price, and the color. Addtionally, a picture of the product is displayed along with the color of the product in the upper right corner.

But that's not all! Makeup-Tracker also has extensive filtering options, allowing you to quickly find products in your collection. This is especially useful for answering questions like "Do I still have that eyeliner?" or "Did I end up buying that Tarte foundation?". Filtering your collection answers those questions quickly!

A vast majority of the supported products are from Ulta. Support for Sephora and other companies will be added in the future. There will also be a way for you to add unsupported products to your own collections yourself!
